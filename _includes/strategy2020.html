Case Two: Video Platform Growth Strategy

Winner: Kyuri Baag

Education: University of Texas at Austin, Class of 2022

Major: B.B.A in Management Information Systems

Kyuri took a holistic approach when tackling this case, and analyzed both the YouTube and TikTok market segments. We were impressed by the in-depth comparison of user segments who use both products, the side-by-side analysis of top accounts on both platforms, and the strategies influencers use to engage both audiences on both platforms.
