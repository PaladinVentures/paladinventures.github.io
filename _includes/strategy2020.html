2020 Product Strategy Case Winners


Case One: EdTech Go-To-Market Strategy

Winner: Noah Cortez

Education: Stanford University

Major: Dual BA and BS, International Relations; Product Design; minor in Modern Languages (Arabic and Tagalog)

Noah's did a thorough analysis of the Orange County market complete with proof of concept's at specific schools. He coupled this with an idea to create a summer immersion experience for students with higher willingness to pay, in addition to our classroom curriculum offering.

Given that the education market is whale heavy (a small percentage of families have an extremely high willingness to pay which constitutes most of the market), creating a premium summer experience for the “whales” was a great idea to increase revenue.
