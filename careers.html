INTERNSHIPS @ PALADIN


 
 

WHO CAN APPLY

At Paladin, we value drive and initiative over past experience. For that reason, we welcome everyone from freshmen to seniors to apply for our internships. Students of all majors are encouraged to apply.

 

PAYMENT

Our internships are paid, however the payment structure varies by role. Payment is some form of commission or profit sharing for the business roles or fixed payment per completed assignment/project for design and engineering roles. Most of our internships for this summer are remote.

 

EXPERIENCE

If you are looking to maximize earnings this summer, PV is most likely not the place for you. Given our portfolio is comprised of early stage startups, we are unable to offer the most competitive compensation packages. However, if your goal is to maximize your personal learning by working with industry leaders with previous experience at top companies, this is definitely the place for you.

ROLES


 

BUSINESS


  • Communications/PR Intern: This role will entail creating a communication and press release strategy for a portfolio company for external messaging to news organization and partners.
  • Social Media & Content Marketing Intern: This role will entail crafting messaging on social media and content creation for a portfolio company.
  • Market Research & Strategy Intern: One of our portfolio companies is working on a disruptive new product which could fundamentally change the curriculum taught in high schools around the country. This internship will entail doing market research to understand the needs of relevant stakeholders and creating a go to market strategy.
  • Growth & Performance Marketing Intern: This role will entail crafting and executing on the paid advertising strategy for a portfolio company on Google, Facebook, and other relevant consumer platforms.

DESIGN & ENGINEERING


  • Product Design Intern: This role will entail creating website and app designs for new verticals being explored by our portfolio companies. Please include links or attachments of your previous design work in your application if applicable.
  • Graphic Design Intern: This role will entail helping graphically shape the brand identity of a portfolio company. Please include links or attachments of your previous design work in your application if applicable.
  • Software Engineering Intern: This role will entail building proof of concepts of product ideas. Available projects include full-stack web development, mobile (iOS or Android) app development, and proof-of-concept algorithm design and testing. Please include links to your Github or other previous projects if applicable.



 
 

APPLY

To apply, email your resume to talent@paladin.ventures with the title of the email being the role you are interested in. You may apply to up to two different roles. Please specify the order of your preference amongst the two roles in the body of the email. You should expect to hear back within 5-7 business days if we will be moving forward with your candidacy.
